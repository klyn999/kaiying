About Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande is an American singer, songwriter, and actress, known for her powerful voice, impressive vocal range, and her significant influence in the music industry. Born on June 26, 1993, in Boca Raton, Florida, she rose to fame as a pop and R&B artist, capturing the hearts of millions with her emotive ballads and upbeat tracks.
